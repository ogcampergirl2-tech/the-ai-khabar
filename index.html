A note from the editor

I started The AI Khabar for a simple reason: most AI coverage is either a hype reel or a research paper, and there's almost nothing in between for the rest of us. The curious, the just-getting-started, the people who want to understand this moment without pretending to be experts.

So that's the whole promise. Honest, clear, weekly. If a thing matters, I'll tell you why. If it doesn't, I'll tell you that too.
