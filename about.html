About

Khabar means news. We bring you the AI kind, made human.

The AI Khabar is a weekly newsletter and blog for people who want to understand artificial intelligence without a computer-science degree or a tolerance for hype.

Why this exists

There's a strange gap in how AI gets written about. On one side: viral threads promising that everything will change tomorrow. On the other: dense research papers and engineering blogs written for people who already know. For everyone in between, the curious, the career-switchers, the just-getting-started, there's surprisingly little.

The AI Khabar lives in that gap. The goal is simple: help you understand what's happening in AI, why it matters, and how to actually use it, in clear language, once a week.

Who it's for

If you're planning to get into AI, thinking about a career move, building a product, or you're just plain curious about the technology reshaping the world, this is written for you. No prior knowledge assumed. No gatekeeping. Just a friendly, honest guide each week.

What you can expect

Every weekly issue carries three things: The Signal (the stories that mattered, in plain English), Khabar Explains (one concept unpacked from scratch), and Try This (something to actually do). Between issues, the blog goes deeper on the ideas worth more than a paragraph.

Members who go Pro get the mid-week Deep Dive, guided build-alongs, the full archive, and a direct line for questions.

Who's behind it

The AI Khabar is an independent publication, written by someone who got tired of explaining the same AI concepts over and over to friends and family, and decided to write them down properly instead.
